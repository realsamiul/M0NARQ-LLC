HawkEYE

30-minute flood mapping

SAR + Optical + DEM fusion with SegFormer-B2-lite yields pixel-accurate flood extents and confidence maps.

Data stack

SAR (Sentinel‑1)

VV backscatter thresholds reveal inundation through water-induced darkening.

Optical (Sentinel‑2)

NDWI highlights open water; SWIR bands suppress vegetation confounders.

DEM (SRTM/30 m)

Low slope priors reduce false positives in steep terrain.

Model & fusion

Three-channel fusion (SAR VV, NDWI, slope) feeds a SegFormer-B2-lite backbone; CRF post-processing sharpens boundaries and removes speckle.
